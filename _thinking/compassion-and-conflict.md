## At first

- Adopt a **learning stance**.
- Practice **Active Listening**.
- Recognise the **common humanity**.

## And then

- Find the feeling at the **heart of the matter**. See presenting issues as a window.
- **Suspend judgement**.
- Find the **common ground**.
