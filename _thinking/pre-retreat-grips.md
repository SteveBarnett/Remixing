- **Mind the gap**
	- Notice that you're lost in thought
	- Notice the gaps between thoughts, between thought and adding a story / judgement
	- Keep widening the gap
- **Do Not Touch**
	- Put it down
	- Let it go
	- Leave it there
- **Say yes** and **relax, rest**, at least in **the gaps**.
