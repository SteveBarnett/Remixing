The Critic and the Coach are two sides of the same coin, two ends of the same bar magnet.

The Critic The Coach
Don't get worse Keep getting better
That wasn't enough That was an improvement
You should have done better You can do better
You're not good enough You're not good enough
Looks at what I didn't do Looks at what I did
Looks down on Looks up at

The Compassionate Observer holds these two (seeming) opposites together.
