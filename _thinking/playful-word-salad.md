## Playful

As in: cheerful, flirtatious, frisky, funny, lively, mischievous, spirited, whimsical.

## Energetic

As in: active, dynamic, lively.

## Mischievous

As in: impish, naughty, frolicsome, rascally.

## Frisky

As in: active, bouncy, peppy, spirited.
