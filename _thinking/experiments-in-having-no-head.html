--- layout: page title: Experiments in Having No Head added: 2024-02-13 updated: 2024-02-13 ---
  • Follow the path up your body. Notice it fades out into open space.
    • Look for your head. Are you behind something? Is that what you see?
  • Your true nature is boundless, open, space.
    • The space says yes to everything.
  • Anything you perceive reveals itself in this empty, still, space.
    • Everything you can perceive appears in your field of perception.
    • Can you see anything that's not in your field of vision?
    • The world is in you.
  • Can you find any gaps between perceptions?
    • Between movement and stillness?
    • Between sounds and silence?
    • Between and not-seen?
